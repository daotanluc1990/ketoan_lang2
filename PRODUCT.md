# Product

## Register

product

## Users

CEO, ke toan, admin, quan ly cua hang va bep trung tam cua chuoi F&B Com Tam Lang. Ho dung app trong boi canh van hanh noi bo hang ngay: kiem doanh thu, tien, kho, cong no, luong, bao cao va dieu kien chot.

## Product Purpose

ERP mini ke toan - tai chinh - kho - bao cao cho chuoi Com Tam Lang. Thanh cong nghia la nguoi dung nhin duoc tinh hinh tot/xau trong vai giay, biet nguon du lieu nao thieu, viec nao can xu ly, va chot bao cao co du dieu kien hay phai chot co ngoai le.

## Brand Personality

Am, chuyen nghiep, thuc dung. Giao dien can co chat F&B Viet Nam va mau do thuong hieu, nhung uu tien do ro, toc do doc so lieu va niem tin vao du lieu hon trang tri.

## Anti-references

Khong giong landing page SaaS, khong dashboard ngan hang lanh leo, khong card mosaic trang tri, khong tab rieng cho canh bao/doi soat/AI, khong bao cao sach gia khi thieu du lieu.

## Design Principles

1. Bang du lieu la noi lam viec chinh.
2. Canh bao phai dan den hanh dong, owner va deadline.
3. Thieu du lieu phai noi ro nguon thieu va cach bo sung.
4. Giao dien desktop uu tien mat do cao nhung khong lam mat ngu canh.
5. Tai lieu va AI ho tro quy trinh, khong thay the module nghiep vu.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Huong toi WCAG AA cho text chinh, focus state ro rang, khong truyen nghia chi bang mau, cac bang lon phai doc duoc voi zoom desktop va co empty/error state bang ngon ngu nghiep vu.
